Welcome to Intelligent Toilet Paper.

How many times you wanted to go to the bathroom and you see that there is not toilet paper? We find this problem very annoying in terms of user experience. This is the reason why we developed a system where you will never run out of toilet paper.

How does it work?

The product consist on a machine where you will put all the toilet paper you have and it will have a sensor that detects when the toilet paper runs out and it will change it automatical. 

* Easy installation
* USB connector to charge it
* Easy to use

Beneficts

You will never run out of toilet paper again, it will make easier to keep the toilet paper in place.
